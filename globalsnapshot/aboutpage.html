About

2016 has been one of the most geopolitically significant years in modern history, with major events playing out across the globe every day. And even though news stories have saturated media outlets, finding a source for news that is both trusted and easily digestible can still be challenging.

The New York Times Global Snapshot geographically maps the most popular New York Times stories from the home page and world section on an interactive webpage. Instantly see the most popular news playing out across the globe, rather than sifting through a list of articles. Why not visualize the news, instead of just reading it?

Resources: New York Times Top Stories API Google Maps Javascript APIGoogle Maps Geolocation API Google AppEngine
